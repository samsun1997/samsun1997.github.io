Description

In this survey, you are invited to make a subjective assessment of some of the intersections.

The survey is divided into the following steps:

1.Watch the intersection video below.
While watching the video, please evaluate the order of the intersection through the following two steps.

2.According to your subjective impression of the order of the intersection in the video, drag the slider below to the location you think is appropriate.
Here is an example of a drag bar:
      Most disorderlyMost orderly

3.Select an option that you think will reflect the order of the intersection appropriately.
Here is an example of an option:
      Most disorderly       More disorderly       Medium       More orderly       Most orderly

4.Make sure you have evaluated all the intersections.Click the "Generate verification code" button below to generate your verification code and copy it to the input box on the original page.

If you already know the survey well, start watching the video below and make your own subjective assessment.
